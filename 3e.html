Networked paging and audio systems allow more effective communication in hospitals, transport hubs, corporate campuses schools and other high-traffic public spaces.

Background music can be used for paging without having to buy extra equipment. A background music and paging system can:
- Provide versatile music content.
- Offer flexible announcements and presentations.
- Distribute high-quality audio.
- Manage audio sources and zone levels using mobile devices or wall controllers.
- Render the surroundings more pleasant by using the system for background music while it's on standby.
