Cafeterias & Town Halls

Town Halls (or all-hands meetings) have become an important way for distributed organisations to engage employees, discuss important topics and achieve team alignment. Whether it’s an in-person or virtual town hall, businesses face the challenge of creating the space for their entire organisation to participate in these interactions.

Broadcast quality stage setup works well for investor meetings, media briefings in addition to town halls. Improved audience engagement with large primary and smaller secondary displays which are visible from anywhere.

Quick switchability between Cafeteria to Town hall/Live Event mode helps facilities teams save setup time. Creates a better environment in Cafeteria mode with live TV, digital signage, background music and paging capabilities.

Live streaming and recording is possible for all events in the space, with cloud based storage allowing on-demand access too.The Multi-camera video capture with auto tracking makes it more comfortable for presenters to move around.
